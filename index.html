MAIRA QUINONES

Biography

Hi! My name is Maira Quinones. I have lived in Denver, Colorado for about six years. I was born in Dallas, Texas and moved to Tampa, Florida when I turned fifteen years old. I would have to say although scary and stressful it was a much needed move. I graduated high school and didn't have a set direction as to where I wanted to take my life. So I joined the Navy and was stationed in Virginia. I was lucky enough to earn a position in the Joint Forces Flag Staff Team. The Flag aka spoiled community to many. Flag Staff Community team is a small community within the Navy that works with the higher chain of command. SO yes I was lucky and my experience was amazing. After the Navy I moved back to Florida where I earned my degree in Legal Studies, I never pursued a career in the field although I loved it to the core. I moved to Colorado in 2016 where I then began a career in Education. I taught multiple levels from 2nd to 8th graders. My favorite grades were middle school. Due to COVID I moved and decided to teach privately and that is what I currently do.

I am a mom to a wonderful eleven year old girl. Who would live in water if she had a choice. I have two younger siblings who are close to me. I have moved them around with me but they have finally decided to make Colorado home for themselves. Most of my family still live in Texas or Mexico. It has been years since I have visited either place.

Why software development for me? I have friends in the field and truly it's how my interest grew. I decided to take the step to pursue it to change my career permanently as I see a vast of avenues a career in software development can bring.
